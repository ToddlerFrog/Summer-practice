Explore the beauty of our finest artworks

Our museum's artwork page has a curated selection of our most notable pieces and collections. Featuring detailed descriptions and high-quality images, it offers insights into the artistry and history behind each work. Enjoy a virtual tour of our collection from the comfort of your home.

Current expositions

Kandinsky

Step into the vibrant, abstract universe of Wassily Kandinsky at this stunning exhibition! Explore his revolutionary use of color, form, and emotion through iconic masterpieces that shaped modern art. From bold geometric compositions to dreamlike improvisations, each work reveals Kandinsky’s genius and his belief in art’s spiritual power.

van Gogh

Experience the passion and brilliance of Vincent van Gogh at this unforgettable exhibition! Marvel at his iconic swirls of Starry Night, glowing Sunflowers, and vivid landscapes that pulse with emotion. Discover the turbulent genius behind the brushstrokes—his dreams, struggles, and unparalleled vision that transformed art forever.

Malevich

Dive into the bold, revolutionary art of Kazimir Malevich, the pioneer of abstract geometric forms. This exhibition showcases his iconic Black Square—a symbol of artistic rebellion—alongside striking Suprematist works that shattered traditional boundaries. Explore how Malevich reduced painting to pure feeling, using stark shapes and vibrant colors to create a new visual language.

Future collections

European Art

Journey through centuries of creativity in this breathtaking exhibition of European masterpieces! From the luminous Renaissance portraits to the dramatic Baroque scenes, the dreamy Impressionist landscapes to the bold strokes of Modernism—discover the evolution of art across time and cultures.

Classical Art

Step into the golden age of antiquity with this exquisite collection of Greek and Roman masterpieces. Discover the perfect proportions of marble gods, the dramatic storytelling of mythological frescoes, and the delicate beauty of Hellenistic sculptures. From powerful athletes to graceful goddesses, each work embodies the classical ideals of balance, beauty and human perfection that inspired artists for centuries.

Abstract Art

Dive into a world where colors, shapes, and emotions break free from tradition! This exhibition celebrates the revolutionary power of abstract art—from Kandinsky’s vibrant compositions to Pollock’s dynamic drips. Discover how artists abandoned realism to express pure energy, rhythm, and feeling.
